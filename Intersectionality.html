Intersectionality

Initially, my two interests seem opposed to each other, but video games and sustainability can actually work together to help people. Many video games promote sustainability through their stories, like Final Fantasy VII. While others support the environmental financially, such as Spilled or Loddlenaut. Gaming also uses a lot of energy, so sustainability, like water and energy conservation, is something that should start to be more considered in the industry. Gamifying more environmentally-friendly choices can improve people's likelihood to participate in them. Overall, both interests improve my design, project management, research, leadership, and communication skills. At the end of the day, game development and sustainability are all about story-telling.
